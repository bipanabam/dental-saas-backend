Engine Architecture

Stop managing disconnected records. DentoCore treats the dental journey as a unified operational pipeline where every database entity automatically mutates and instantiates downstream clinical events.

Deterministic Workflow Integrity

Data models shouldn't live in silos. DentoCore maps physical workflows natively into relational database patterns. When a clinical action occurs, schema states update automatically across modules.

Event-Driven Lifecycle Updates

Immutable Audit Timelines
